Search our knowledgebase

  • How do I mine?

    Mine Gridcoin the same as you would mine litecoin. Download cg-miner 3.10 or GuiMiner scrypt alpha. Set up your config file to solo mine.

  • How do I send Gridcoins?

    Ask the recipient for their gridcoin address. Press the Send button from the main form. Enter the recipients wallet address and fill out the form and press Send.
